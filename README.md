# 📡 WaveCon Telecom 5G Impact Analysis | Power BI

## 📌 Project Overview

This project analyzes the impact of WaveCon Telecom's 5G launch on business performance across key metrics such as Revenue, ARPU (Average Revenue Per User), Active Users, Customer Churn, Market Share, and Plan Performance.

Using Power BI, interactive dashboards were created to evaluate how the introduction of 5G services affected customer behavior, revenue generation, and product adoption across different cities and telecom plans.

---

## 🎯 Business Problem

WaveCon launched its 5G services in June 2022 with the goal of improving customer experience and driving business growth.

Management wanted to understand:

* Did 5G increase revenue?
* How did customer engagement change after the rollout?
* Which plans performed well after 5G?
* Which plans should be revised or discontinued?
* How did market share change after 5G adoption?

## 📈 Dashboard Pages

### 1️⃣ Revenue Analysis

Analyzed revenue performance before and after the 5G launch.

#### Key Findings

* Revenue before 5G: ₹16.0 Billion
* Revenue after 5G: ₹15.9 Billion
* Overall Change: -0.50%

#### Insight

The 5G rollout did not immediately increase revenue. However, revenue remained relatively stable despite the transition period.

---

### 2️⃣ ARPU Analysis

Measured changes in Average Revenue Per User after the 5G launch.

#### Key Findings

* Before 5G: ₹190.2
* After 5G: ₹211.3
* Growth: +11.05%

#### Insight

Customers began spending more after adopting 5G services, indicating strong monetization potential through premium plans.

---

### 3️⃣ Active User Analysis

Evaluated customer engagement before and after 5G implementation.

#### Key Findings

* Before 5G: 84.4 Million Users
* After 5G: 77.4 Million Users
* Change: -8.28%

#### Insight

A significant decline in active users suggests adoption challenges or customer dissatisfaction during the transition phase.

---

### 4️⃣ Customer Churn Analysis

Analyzed unsubscribed users before and after the 5G launch.

#### Key Findings

* Before 5G: 5.6 Million
* After 5G: 7.0 Million
* Increase: 23.50%

#### Insight

Customer churn increased significantly after the rollout, highlighting potential issues related to pricing, network experience, or migration challenges.

---

### 5️⃣ Market Share Analysis

Compared WaveCon's market share against competitors.

#### Competitors

* Britel
* Dadafone
* PIO
* Other Telecom Operators

#### Insight

Market share analysis helps identify competitive positioning and opportunities for customer acquisition.

---

### 6️⃣ City-Level Revenue Analysis

Analyzed revenue performance across major cities.

#### Top Revenue Cities

1. Mumbai
2. Delhi
3. Kolkata
4. Bangalore
5. Chennai

#### Lowest Revenue Cities

1. Raipur
2. Gurgaon
3. Chandigarh
4. Coimbatore
5. Patna

#### Insight

Metro cities continue to contribute the majority of telecom revenue.

## 📱 Plan Performance Analysis

### Best Performing Plan

#### Plan P1

* Highest revenue-generating plan
* Consistent growth after the 5G launch
* Revenue increased from ₹448M to ₹628M

**Recommendation:** Continue promoting and enhancing this plan.

---

### Stable Plans

#### Plan P2 & Plan P3

* Minimal impact from the 5G rollout
* Revenue remained relatively stable

**Recommendation:** Maintain current offerings while monitoring future performance.

---

### Newly Introduced Plans

#### Plans P11, P12, and P13

Introduced after the 5G launch.

##### Highlights

* P11 demonstrated strong adoption.
* P12 showed stable performance.
* P13 generated comparatively lower revenue.

---

## ⚠️ Underperforming Plans

### Plan P4

* Gradual revenue decline
* Requires feature enhancements

### Plan P5

* Revenue dropped by approximately 36%
* Still retains a sizeable customer base

**Recommendation:** Revise pricing and benefits rather than discontinuing.

### Plan P6

* Consistent decline post-launch

### Plan P7

* Revenue collapsed by more than 70%
* Significant customer migration observed

**Recommendation:** Discontinue or completely redesign this plan.

---

## 🚫 Discontinued Plans Analysis

The following plans disappeared after the 5G launch:

### P8 – Daily Saviour

Likely discontinued due to:

* Low data allowance
* Short validity period
* Incompatibility with 5G usage patterns

### P9 – Combo Top-Up

Likely discontinued because:

* Limited data offering
* Low customer demand

### P10 – Big Combo

Likely discontinued due to:

* Low revenue contribution
* Short-term value proposition

---

## 💡 Key Business Insights

✅ ARPU increased significantly after the 5G launch.

✅ Revenue remained stable despite a major technology transition.

✅ Customer churn increased by 23.5%.

✅ Active users declined by over 8%.

✅ Plan P1 emerged as the strongest post-5G product.

✅ Plans P5 and P7 require immediate strategic action.

✅ Metro cities continue to drive the majority of business revenue.
